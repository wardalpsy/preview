Psychologist, psychotherapist, and hypnotherapist, registered with the [Professional Order of Psychologists](https://www.psicologipuglia.it/single-professionista/?id=7134) of the Puglia Region (Italy). I specialized in Ericksonian psychotherapy and hypnosis at SIIPE in Rome.

I gained my clinical experience in the mental health sector within public and hospital services (such as Addiction Services, Mental Health Centers, and polyclinics), working with addiction, depression, grief, trauma, anxiety, and somatic symptoms. I have over 10 years of experience working with the expatriate community, as well as in the fields of education, sports, performance, and organizational settings.

My therapeutic work is conducted in strict accordance with the Code of Ethics for Italian Psychologists and is supported by continuous clinical supervision.

I am particularly passionate about anthropology, nature, music, dance, and dogs. In my free time, I love observing everything around me, discovering and exploring new things, and baking sweets :)."
