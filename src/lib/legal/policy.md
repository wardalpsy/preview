### 2. Zero-Retention Policy

Unlike traditional websites, we do not maintain a database of your information.

- When you fill out a contact or consent form, the data is processed in real-time by a secure "serverless" function (Cloudflare).
- The information is immediately encrypted and transmitted to the Doctor’s secure professional email via our delivery provider (Resend).
- Once the transmission is complete, the data is deleted from our web environment.

### 3. Personal & Health Data We Process

We only process the information you voluntarily provide:

- **Contact Info:** Name, Surname, Email, Phone Number.
- **Request Info:** Subject and message content.
- **Health Data:** Any information you provide regarding your condition is treated with strict medical confidentiality.
- **Consent Records:** A digital record of your signed consent form is generated and sent to both you and the Doctor.

### 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Consent (Art. 6 & 9 GDPR):** For contact requests and health-related booking, we rely on your explicit, voluntary consent.
- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the professional services you are requesting.

### 5. Third-Party Service Providers

To ensure a secure and high-performance experience, we use a limited number of "Sub-Processors" who are also GDPR compliant:

- **Cloudflare (Hosting & Security):** Hosts the website logic and protects against attacks.
- **Resend (Email Delivery):** Securely transmits your form data to the Doctor.
- **Stripe (Payments):** Processes payments. We never see or store your credit card details.
- **Cal.com (Scheduling):** Manages the booking calendar.

### 6. Cookie Policy

We do not use any tracking, advertising, or profiling cookies. We do not use Google Analytics.

We only use **one (1) Strictly Necessary Cookie**:

- **Name:** `session_auth` (or similar)
- **Purpose:** This cookie is only set **after** you enter the password provided by the Doctor to access the Reserved Area. It is used solely to keep you logged in while you complete your booking.
- **Duration:** This is a session cookie and expires when you close your browser.

_Note: Because this cookie is strictly necessary for the service you requested, a cookie consent banner is not required by law._

### 7. Data Storage & Security

While the website itself stores nothing, the final destination of your data is the Doctor’s professional email inbox. This email is secured with:

- End-to-end encryption during transit (TLS/SSL).
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- Professional medical data retention standards.

- Access the data you provided (by requesting a copy of the email record).
- Request the erasure of your data.
- Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact the Doctor directly at **[Insert Email]**.
